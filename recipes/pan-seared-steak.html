Pan Seared Steak

Ingredients

  • Rib Eye Steaks
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Mustard
  • Bourbon

Recipe

  1. Season steak with salt at least 45 min prior to cooking. (gets moisture out and properly seasons meat.)
  2. Heat oil up ripping hot and cook steak 30 seconds each side and keep flippping until thermometer reads 125 F
  3. For pan sauce, take some hearty mustard, bourbon, garlic and cook down so it leaves streaks after running a wooden spoon through it.
