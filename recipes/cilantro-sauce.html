Cilantro Lime Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup cilantro
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup mayo
  • 1/4 cup pickled jalepenos
  • 2 Tbsp fresh lime juice
  • 1 green onion
  • 1 large garlic clove
  • A pinch of salt

Recipe

  1. Place all ingredients into a food processor and blend until smooth.
  2. Test for taste and add as you please.
