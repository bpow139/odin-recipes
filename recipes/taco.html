Tacos

Ingredients

  • Taco shells
  • 1 lbs ground beef (80/20)
  • Taco seasoning
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Sour cream
  • Salsa

Recipe

  1. Cook ground beef until starting to turn color
  2. Add in taco seasoning and also some water to keep from drying.
  3. After beef is done cooking, assemple tacos as you please with the rest of the ingredients.
